Space Walls

Endless mobile game

Made with Unity
Google Play Services
Sound design
Graphic design

Space Walls is an endless mobile game, developed using Unity 3D for Android and iOS. The main goal is to move your "ship" to the correct side of the space walls coming at you. The walls get faster and faster and your "ship" changes shape by the time.

There are some special walls which give players coins. With these coins, they can buy a wide variety of skins which they can use for the walls, as well for the "ship".

Google Play Services are implemented in this game, which means users can compete with their friends and check who has the highest score in the leaderboard.

All the sprites, textures, 3D models and the background song and sounds were designed and made by myself.
