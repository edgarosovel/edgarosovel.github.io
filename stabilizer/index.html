Camera stabilizer

Arduino powered camera stabilizer

Arduino
Embedded system

This camera stabilizer was developed using an Arduino, a MPU6050 accelerometer/gyroscope and 2 servomotors.

Even though the servos do a great job to counter the movement, they are not very precise.

Lesson learned: should have used stepper motors instead!
